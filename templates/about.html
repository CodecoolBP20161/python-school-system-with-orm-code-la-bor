About

Founded in late 2014, we have launched our first course in September 2015. This means that 40+ students will be available for work in the second half of 2016. The next course starts in Budapest in early 2016. Poland and Spain follow a few months later. In 3 years more than 500 Codecool graduates will be working in the industry in Europe.

Codecool is a new approach to teach computer programming. Presented with real life projects, students graduate with experience required by the market. Codecool is a private school founded to fill a niche in the IT sector by teaching young people to code in just 18 months. During the full-time educational process the students gain market ready experience which enables them to hit the job market immediately.

Codecool's mission is to create international IT education centers that train experienced, market ready software developers in quarter or third time compared to higher educational institutes.
